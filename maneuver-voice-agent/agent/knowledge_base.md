## About Maneuver

AI consultancy helping 10–200-person companies make the AI shift. Clients: founders without a CTO, won't sign Big Four contracts, need practical AI that ties to real business metrics. No jargon, no six-month discovery, no AI for AI's sake.

## Services

**AI Readiness Sprint (ai-readiness):** 2-week diagnostic turning "we should do something with AI" into a costed, prioritized roadmap. Best for companies exploring AI but unsure where to start.

**Agentic AI (agentic-ai):** Workflow agents that take real work off your team's plate — deployed in 4–6 weeks. Handles order processing, dispatch, status tracking, exception handling, and more.

**Voice AI Concierge (voice-ai):** Arabic + English voice agents handling up to 80% of customer inquiries automatically across phone, WhatsApp, and messaging channels. 24/7 availability.

**Fractional CTO (fractional-cto):** Ownership of your tech strategy, vendor decisions, and AI roadmap without a full-time hire. Weekly syncs, async access, hands-on guidance.

## Process

1. **Discovery call** (30 min) — understand the business, current pain, and decision timeline.
2. **Diagnostic & scope** (3–5 days) — map workflows, identify AI opportunities, define engagement shape.
3. **Build & deploy** (2–6 weeks) — focused sprints with visible progress, async updates, live reviews.
4. **Handoff & support** — documentation, training, and 30-day support window post-launch.

## Pricing

AI Readiness Sprint: flat fee engagement. Agentic AI and Voice AI: project-based, scoped after diagnostic. Fractional CTO: monthly retainer. No hourly billing.

## Case Studies

**Freight Brokerage (freight-brokerage):** Manual freight ops created bottlenecks across dispatch, tracking, and customer comms. We deployed intelligent automation for order intake, carrier matching, dispatch generation, and real-time status tracking with proactive exception alerts. Result: 3+ hrs recovered per dispatcher per day, zero manual status updates, deployed in 4 weeks. Decoupled headcount growth from shipment volume.

**Hospitality Group (hospitality-group):** Multi-property vacation rental group in Dubai running on spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and disconnected tools. We built a complete guest operations platform with AI concierge across WhatsApp, Airbnb, and Booking.com, automated guest journey messaging, multi-level escalation, and operations dashboard. Result: 80% of guest communication handled automatically, 3 channels unified, 24/7 AI concierge. Properties scale without proportional staff growth.

**Industrial Supplier (industrial-supplier):** Supplier and customer communication scattered across WhatsApp, calls, and email with no centralized system. We built a unified communication layer with WhatsApp automation and Voice AI agent handling orders, confirmations, and status inquiries. Result: single source of truth for all orders, 3 channels consolidated to 1, 60%+ reduction in manual data entry.
