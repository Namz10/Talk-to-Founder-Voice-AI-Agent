## About Maneuver

Maneuver is a boutique product strategy and design studio founded in 2019. We work with funded startups and scaleups that need sharper product direction, better user experience, and faster execution without adding a large internal team. The studio is intentionally small, with about 12 people across strategy, design, engineering, research, and growth.

## Services

### Product Discovery Sprint

- Name: product-discovery
- Description: A focused sprint that turns a vague opportunity, customer pain, or feature idea into a validated product direction. We interview stakeholders, map assumptions, prioritize risks, and leave the team with a clear MVP scope and decision-ready roadmap.
- Typical engagement length: 2 weeks
- Ideal client type: Seed to Series B teams preparing to build, reposition, or validate a new product bet.

### UX & Interface Design

- Name: ux-interface-design
- Description: We design clear, polished product experiences for web and mobile software, moving from flows and wireframes into production-ready interface systems. The work focuses on usability, conversion, and enough visual distinction to feel credible in a crowded market.
- Typical engagement length: 4-8 weeks
- Ideal client type: Startups with an existing product that feels hard to use, dated, or inconsistent.

### Brand Identity System

- Name: brand-identity-system
- Description: We create practical identity systems for companies that need to look trustworthy before a launch, fundraise, or market expansion. The output usually includes positioning, logo direction, color, type, messaging pillars, and reusable brand rules.
- Typical engagement length: 3-6 weeks
- Ideal client type: Founder-led companies with early traction but an underdeveloped brand.

### MVP Build (React/Next.js)

- Name: mvp-build
- Description: We build investor-ready and customer-ready MVPs using React, Next.js, and modern backend integrations. The emphasis is on shipping the smallest serious version that can support real usage, feedback, and sales conversations.
- Typical engagement length: 6-12 weeks
- Ideal client type: Funded startups that need to launch quickly without hiring a full product team first.

### Design System Creation

- Name: design-system-creation
- Description: We create scalable design systems that reduce product inconsistency and speed up delivery across design and engineering. This includes component libraries, usage rules, token structures, and documentation that teams can actually maintain.
- Typical engagement length: 4-10 weeks
- Ideal client type: Scaleups with multiple product surfaces, repeated UI debt, or growing design and engineering teams.

### Growth & Conversion Optimization

- Name: growth-conversion-optimization
- Description: We improve activation, onboarding, signup, trial, and purchase flows through research-backed experiments and better interface decisions. The work blends analytics, UX, copy, and rapid design iteration around measurable business outcomes.
- Typical engagement length: 4-8 weeks
- Ideal client type: SaaS and marketplace teams with traffic or users but underperforming conversion.

### Technical Architecture Review

- Name: technical-architecture-review
- Description: We review product architecture, frontend structure, integration choices, and delivery risks before a major build or rebuild. The output is a practical technical plan with risks, tradeoffs, sequencing, and team recommendations.
- Typical engagement length: 1-3 weeks
- Ideal client type: Founders, CTOs, and product leads who need an outside read before committing budget or roadmap time.

### Ongoing Retainer (design + strategy)

- Name: ongoing-retainer
- Description: We act as a senior product, design, and strategy partner for teams that need continuous momentum but are not ready to build a full in-house function. Retainers usually cover product direction, design reviews, roadmap shaping, growth experiments, and selective execution.
- Typical engagement length: 3-6 months
- Ideal client type: Funded startups and scaleups that need senior product leverage every week.

## Engagement Process

### Intake call

- Description: We learn the business context, current product challenge, decision timeline, and what success needs to look like.
- Duration: 30-45 minutes

### Proposal & scope

- Description: We turn the opportunity into a clear engagement shape with goals, deliverables, timeline, responsibilities, and investment.
- Duration: 2-4 business days

### Kickoff & discovery

- Description: We align with the team, review existing material, interview key stakeholders, and identify the highest-risk assumptions.
- Duration: 3-7 business days

### Execution sprints

- Description: We work in focused weekly sprints with visible progress, async updates, live reviews, and fast decision cycles.
- Duration: 2-10 weeks depending on scope

### Handoff & support

- Description: We package the work, document key decisions, support implementation, and stay available through launch or transition.
- Duration: 1-2 weeks

## Pricing

Retainers range from $8k-$20k per month depending on scope. Fixed-price projects usually range from $15k-$80k. The Product Discovery Sprint is a $4,500 flat engagement. We do not do hourly billing because it usually creates the wrong incentives for strategic product work.

## Case Studies

### Northstar Ledger

- Client: Northstar Ledger
- Problem: The company had strong early demand for its finance automation product, but new users were abandoning onboarding before connecting their accounting data.
- Solution: Maneuver redesigned the onboarding flow, clarified the product promise, and created a progressive setup experience that let users see value before completing every integration.
- Outcome: Trial activation increased by 38%, onboarding completion improved from 42% to 67%, and sales-assisted demos shortened by one full call on average.

### LumaHire

- Client: LumaHire
- Problem: The recruiting platform had enterprise interest, but the product looked too lightweight for buyers evaluating it against larger incumbents.
- Solution: Maneuver rebuilt the core dashboard experience, introduced a more credible brand identity system, and designed a reporting layer for hiring leaders.
- Outcome: The team closed two mid-market pilots within 60 days, increased average contract value by 24%, and reduced custom demo preparation time by about 40%.

### CarePilot

- Client: CarePilot
- Problem: CarePilot was preparing for a seed extension and needed to prove that its care coordination concept could work as a usable MVP.
- Solution: Maneuver ran a discovery sprint, defined the MVP scope, built a React prototype, and then shipped a production pilot with secure role-based workflows.
- Outcome: The pilot supported 11 clinic users in the first month, reduced manual coordination tasks by 31%, and helped the company secure a $1.8M seed extension.

## FAQ

### Do you only work with funded companies?

Mostly, yes. We are a good fit when there is enough budget and urgency to make product decisions quickly, although we occasionally work with bootstrapped teams that have strong traction.

### Can you work with our existing engineering team?

Yes. Many engagements are design, strategy, or architecture-led, where our job is to help your internal engineers move faster with clearer direction and better product decisions.

### Do you build production software or only prototypes?

We do both. Discovery and design engagements may end with prototypes, while MVP Build engagements are intended to ship real production software.

### How quickly can an engagement start?

Most engagements can start within two to three weeks after scope approval. Smaller discovery sprints can sometimes start sooner if calendars line up.

### What makes Maneuver different from a typical agency?

Maneuver is smaller and more senior than a typical agency. Clients work directly with experienced product strategists, designers, and engineers instead of a large account team.
